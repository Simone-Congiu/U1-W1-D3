Focaccia with caramelized onion,cranberries, and brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizertoppedwith sweetcaramelizedonions,tar cranberries, and creamy brie.It will becomeyour new favoriteholiday starter!

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500gr) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/ teaspoon salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350gr) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium ywllow onions,thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegear
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushingon top
  • 10 ounce brie,cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife is desired, tought it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dought

In a medium sized mixing bowl,or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix togheter all the dought
ingredients until the flouris fully absorbed.
